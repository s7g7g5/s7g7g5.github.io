About our Company

Delwin Industries is a sheet metal manufacturing unit. Delwin Industries fabricates Stainless Steel and Mild Steel products. It also provide powder coating services.

Our Key Principles:

  1. Innovation

  3. Technology Driven

Our Production Methodology:

All the products manufactured/fabricated at Delwin industries are designed with precision & powder coated with high quality material procured from well-established vendors. All products are manufactured in strict accordance with the standard industry parameters. Our products can also be customized as per the specific requirements of the clients. In addition to manufacturing, we also offer services like Fabrication, Power Coating etc. at competitive rates to our clients.

Our Quality Standards:

To ensure all our production services are streamlined, we have developed a vast infrastructure well equipped with latest machinery and technologies. We have expert professionals, which enables us to meet the production targets within stipulated time. We lay great emphasis on the quality of the products and thereby test our products on industry defined parameters to assure our clients receives quality products. For safe delivery, our finished products are packed in quality packaging or as required by the clients.
